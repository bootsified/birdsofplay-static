Birds of Play Soccer Update
posted by Coach Aaron – Tue, February 28th, 2017 @ 4:30am

Hi all,

The season is upon us! Here are a few items to take note of:

Uniforms
There is a really cool screenprinting shop on NW Hwy at Easton (Rebel Riot) that is giving me pricing on screenprinted Birds of Play t-shirts. It is looking like 4-color will be too expensive, so I am going to go with a black shirt with a white logo (similar to attached). They can also do these as adult shirt, so if any adults want a team shirt for themselves, let me know. I will also need you to send over your players' t-shirt sizes ASAP. I will be getting pricing very soon and then I will send out a request for PayPal or Venmo $$. We will also wear some black shorts and black-and-white striped socks. I will have details on those soon.

Skills Camp
This Saturday the YMCA is hosting a skills camp for all league players. It will be at Randall Park from 11:30-12:30. It's not really a team event (all kids are just in one big group) but Hugh and I went last year and it was worthwhile to introduce your kids to the basics (or refresh them).

First Actual Practice
Our first team practice will be next Wednesday (3/8) at Lake Highlands Park at 6pm. Please bring a ball and have your kiddo wear cleats and shin guards. Kindergartners use size 3 balls.

Roster
Our roster is now complete, with 11 players. Anyone whom I previously contacted about not being listed with us can now put any concerns to rest -- we are all squared away.

First Game
Our first game should be on March 11, but time is TBD, as the YMCA hasn't released the schedule yet.

Help
Lastly, please let me know if you are able to help with 1) snack coordination, 2) sideline/subbing help during game, or 3) practice assistance. It sometimes takes a few voices to herd Kindergartners.

Thanks! Please let me know if you have any questions.

Aaron

Spring Soccer
posted by Coach Aaron – Wed, February 8th, 2017 @ 4:00pm

Hello all,

Looks like we've got a dynamite team together. I'm excited to have some old faces and some new. Here are a few initial details:

Registration
If you haven't yet, please register on the White Rock YMCA website (or in person) and Coach Aaron Hobbins down as the team name. -- deadline is this Friday.

Season schedule
Game schedule isn't out yet, but the season runs from March 4-May 13

Practices
Practices will be each Wednesday prior to Saturday games (if there's no game that week, there's no practice that week). We'll meet at 6pm and will practice for about 30 minutes. Hexter is reserved for YMCA use but teams can't make individual reservations and it gets crowded. My preference would be to practice at Lake Highlands Park just down the street from Hexter. The drawback there is that we can only reserve it two weeks at a time. I am looking into other options, but tentatively I would like to take our chances on Lake Highlands Park.

Uniforms
We're going to be opting for a t-shirt with a logo on it this time. I will get this ball rolling shortly and let everyone know.

Mascot/Team Name
We're a hybrid school team so I don't want to use Eagles or Hawks. If you have any creative, non-trademarked ideas, please send them my way, and I'll pick 3 to send out to you all for a vote.

Thanks,

Aaron
